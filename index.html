Hello!
I am Haotian from Parsons School of Design.
Welcome to my New York inspired Visual Archive gallery, Fall 2021.

This is my archive documenting New York City and the things I have made contact with while living and studying here this semester through experimenting with the photos I have taken so far.
Bringing the archive online is a nice way to share it to the world~

As an artist interested in visualised and creative works, I seek to make my work using the methods of double exposure and further image manipulations.
Colors, overlays, dimensions, and photography are what craft this visual archive and they helped me to generate interesting and unique works.
